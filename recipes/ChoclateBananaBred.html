ChoclateBananaBred

Description

This is a very creative and mouth watering banana bread with choclate chips recipe, created with amazing and easy to follow steps that will lead you to a delicious desert!

Ingredients

    1 cup of margarine
    2 cups of white sugar
    4 eggs
    6 Bananas, mashed
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    3 cups all-pupose flour
    2 Teaspoons of baking soda
    1/4 cup of unsweetned cocoa powder
    1 cup liter of sour cream
    1 cup of semisweet choclate chips

Now the moment we've been waitng for, the mouth watering recipe!!

Steps

    Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Lightly grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans
    Step 2:In a large bowl, cream together margarine, sugar and eggs. Stir in bananas and vanilla. Sift in flour, baking soda and coca; moix well. Blend in sour cream and choclate chips. Pour batter in prepared pans
    Step 3:Bake i prehead oven for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a loaf comes out clean

Enjoy! you've made the mouth watering ChoclateBananaBread
