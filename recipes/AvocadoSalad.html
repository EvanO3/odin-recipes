AvocadoSalad

Description

This AvocadoSalad is the an amazing fusion of Greek, Latin and Italian cultures. This salad can help add a healty, tasty and satisfying blend to any lunch or dinner. It is sure to release all endorphines including dopamine that will make you feel great after eating!

Ingredients

    5 medium avocados- halfed, peeled, and pitted
    2 large cucumbers, ends trimmed
    4 medium Roma tomatoes
    1/4 cup italian salad dressing
    1/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves
    3/4 teaspoons freeshly ground black pepper, or to taste
    1/2 teaspoon sea salt, or to taste

Directions

Step 1: Cut the avocadoes into wedges. Peel cucumbers, if desired, and cut into slics. Core tomatoes and cut into wedges. Transfer all into a bowl
    Step 2:Add vinegar, Italian dressing, oregano, pepper, and salt; mix well until combined. Taste and adjust seasoning accordingly
